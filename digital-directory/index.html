The Digital Directory contains a selection of playful websites from across the internet. Socially distance from humans and instead spend quality time reconnecting with non-humans (plant, animal, machine). Remind ourselves that we are embedded in a more-than-human world - and to have some fun along the way. Made in isolation by TERRAIN Projects and Girl on Road for Earth Day's 50th anniversary, 22 April 2020.
